ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm Jonathan Yabes.

I'm a proud father of two, husband to a lovely wife, and dedicated family man.

My passion is telling stories through motion design. I love the whole creative process from developing concepts to pushing keyframes and bringing scenes to life through animation. My aim is always connecting with audience and evoking emotions.

In my spare time, you can find me outdoors enjoying nature or spending time with my family.

I thrive on collaborating with interesting people and taking on new creative challenges. If you're looking for a passionate and dedicated motion design partner, I'd love to hear about your project. Please feel free to drop me a line whatever's on your heart and mind.

Let's create something amazing together!
