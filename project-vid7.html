Guardian Christmas


In this task, we're challenged to apply techniques learned throughout the semester to create a final infomercial video promoting a new brand. The goal is to effectively showcase key elements of the brand in a way that captivates and engages the viewer. Utilizing text call-outs strategically throughout the video adds an interactive element, ensuring the audience remains engaged with the product being promoted.

Tools: After Effects | Photoshop
For: Filmmagik Studio Singapore
Role: Motion Designer | Graphic Artist
